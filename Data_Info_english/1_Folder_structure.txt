Folder structure:

- A_basins_total_upstrm (basin delineation A, see article chapter 3, Fig. 2a)
  - 1_attributes
    - Catchment_attributes.csv
    - Stream_dist.csv
    - Water_balance.csv
  - 2_timeseries
    - daily
      - meteorological time series for 859 catchments
  - 3_shapefiles (EPSG 3035)
    - Basins_A.shp

- B_basins_intermediate_all (basin delineation B, see article chapter 3, Fig. 2b)
  - 1_attributes
    - Catchment_attributes.csv
    - Crossbasin_water_transfers.csv
    - Gauge_hierarchy.csv
    - Stream_dist.csv
  - 2_timeseries
    - daily
      - meteorological time series for 859 (intermediate) catchments
  - 3_shapefiles (EPSG 3035)
    - Basins_B.shp

- C_basins_intermediate_lowimp (basin delineation C, see article chapter 3, Fig. 2c)
  - 1_attributes
    - Catchment_attributes.csv
    - Gauge_hierarchy.csv
    - Stream_dist.csv
  - 2_timeseries
    - daily
      - meteorological time series for 454 (intermediate) catchments
  - 3_shapefiles (EPSG 3035)
    - Basins_C.shp

- D_gauges
  - 1_attributes
    - Gauge_attributes.csv
    - Hydro_indices_1981_2017.csv (calculated for the period from the first 1 October in the individual time series after 1981 to 30 September 2017)
    - Hydro_indices_1989_2009.csv (calculated for the period 1 October 1989 to 30 September 2009)
  - 2_timeseries
    - daily
      - runoff time series for 882 gauges
    - gaps
      - timesteps with gaps before interpolation
  - 3_shapefiles (EPSG 3035)
    - Gauges.shp

- E_river_network (EPSG 3035)
  - _Info.txt
  - EU-Hydro_network.shp
  - RiverATLAS.shp

- F_hydrol_model
  - 1_input
    - various input files
  - 2_timeseries
    - input and output fluxes for 859 (intermediate) catchments
  - 3_shapefiles
    - Hyd_model.shp
    - various styles for visualization of Hyd_model.shp in GIS
  - 4_output
    - parameter_calibrated.txt (calibrated parameter field)
    - statistics_cal.csv (statistics for calibration period)
    - statistics_val.csv (statistics for validation period)
  - Lookup_table_ID_NB.csv (ID - unique identifier in LamaH, NB - unique identifier in the hydrological model COSERO)

- G_appendix
  - 1_attributes
    - EU-Hydro_network_start_end_ID.csv (start- (most distant to the basin outlet) and end- (basin outlet) OBJECT_ID of the river segment from EU-Hydro_network.shp, basinwise)
  - 2_codes
    - Python
      - 1_attributes
      - 2_supplement
      - Info.txt
    - R
      - 1_attributes
      - 2_timeseries
      - 3_plots
      - 4_supplement
  - 3_shapefiles (EPSG 3035)
    - _Info.txt (Information for Impacts.shp)
    - GLiM.shp (intersecting and reprojected polygons from the GLiM dataset)
    - Grid_ERA5L.shp (intersecting grids from the ERA5-Land dataset)
    - Impacts.shp (Localization of impacts based on intermediate catchments)
    - Mask_ctry.shp (Mask for covering country borders)
    - River_regions.shp (River- and Macro-River regions)
    - Subbasins.shp (Subbasins from the Digital Hydrological Atlas of Austria and from RiverATLAS)
  - 4_plots
    - GLiM_classes_colors.txt (color table for Fig. 11a)
    - LamaH logos
  - 5_CORINE_landcover
